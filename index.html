Today I Made a Website
Saturday, September 19, 2015

I finally did it. All this time I've had thoughts and no where to put them. Today is the day I change that. I've decided to code it from scratch, using the experience I gained using bootstrap to make UBC AeroDesign's webpage, and pair it with the Javascript I learned to make hipster-feed (more on that in a future blog post!). It's fun learning to use jQuery to make things like my super-ridiculously-over-the-top menu bar. Seriously, stop readind this and check it our right now. Cool right?

Expect this site to contain my progress and thought processes on my various random projects, and philosophies on anything and everything. The site is still in developement, so a lot of functionality isn't working. Of course, it isn't online right now either -- I'm literally writing a post just to see how the formating will fit in -- but once I do upload the site, it still may not be completed. Who knows who may have stumbled accross this website!

Anyways, I'll keep you all in the loop on my progress. Thanks for checking it, and I hope you enjoy the site!
